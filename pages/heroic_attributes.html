Heroic Attribute Special Action
(done during the move phase) 		Dice Modifier
Might (Mi) Perform a second move, perform a non-described action (e.g. climbing on a monster), etc. To-Hit, To-Wound, Climb, Jump, or any Strength and Fight-related action; boost the Fight value in melee rolls
Will (Wi) Casting spells, forcing an ally henchmen to retry its morale check, Resistant a magical power Spell casting dice, Courage/morale check, Blocking spell dice
Fate (Fa) Blocking an attack (can be done at any time) or spell, reroll a dice roll and keep the new result (e.g. a jump that failed), etc. Any dice from any other heroic attributes can be boosted with Fa
